Admission

Applications are now closed.

The main directions of research in our group include Deep Learning and Bayesian Methods, Optimization in Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Audio Signal Processing, Generative Models, and Deep Learning for Source Code.

Admission process:

  1. Complete the application form online.
  2. Write a scientific report on a topic which we will send you after the 1st step. Report format details.
  3. We will invite you to an oral interview.
  4. The best applicants will be selected to participate in a summer internship. This internship is a kind of probation: you will work on a research project with a curator from our group and we'll see whether we are a good match. The internship may be skipped in extra cases.

The deadline for the scientific report is 23:59 May 30. Please send your report to Ekaterina Volzhenina with the tag [AdmissionReport]. We will organize interviews in the beginning of June and notify the applicants accepted to the internship in June.

If have any questions, please contact Ekaterina Volzhenina. Please, use the tag [BayesGroupAdmission] in a subject of your email.
