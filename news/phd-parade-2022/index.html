PhD Parade 2022

28 Oct 2022

We're happy to announce that several members of our lab have completed and successfully defended their PhD studies:

  • Arsenii Ashukha on 29 Sep 2022 with thesis "Prior Knowledge for Deep Learning"
  • Ekaterina Lobacheva on 30 Oct 2022 with thesis "Deep Learning Architectures on a Limited Memory Budget"
  • Nadezhda Chirkova on 6 Oct 2022 with thesis "Neural Networks for Source Code Processing"
  • Dmitry Molchanov on 28 Oct 2022 with thesis "Doubly Stochastic Variational Inference with Semi-Implicit and Improper Distributions"

Our congratulations to newly baked doctors!
