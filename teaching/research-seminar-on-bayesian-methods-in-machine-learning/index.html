Research Seminar on Bayesian Methods in Machine Learning

Research seminar is the main weekly event for all members of the group. This is where we discuss new papers, present our own findings as well as host fellow researchers from other labs. The seminar is open for anyone to attend, please see the schedule (in Russian) for details. The standard language of the seminar is English, but occasionally we have talks in Russian.

Also, the seminar is recorded, and past recording are available at our YouTube channel.
